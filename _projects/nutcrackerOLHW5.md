Problem Statement: Design a nutcracker to crack open a macadamia nut.
Constraints: The average grip strength of a human is approximately 40kg and this design must account for that. The device must also be an appropriate size such that it is not cumbersome to use.
Approach: To design this nutcracker, it was assumed that the average force to crack open a macadamia nut is approximately 222.18kg. Also, the size of the macadamia nut was assumed to be 2.5cm in diameter. A moment balance about A was performed to determine the necessary distance between A and the location where the force of the hand is applied. It was determined that the force of the hand must be applied 7cm from A.
